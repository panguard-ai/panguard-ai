## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up on the web">
    Go to [panguard.ai](https://panguard.ai) and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.

    You can register with:
    - **Google account** -- one-click OAuth sign-in
    - **Email + password** -- traditional registration with email verification
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Panguard is 100% open source under the MIT license. No account or credit card required.

    <Info>
      All features are included. No paid tiers, no paywalls. Visit [panguard.ai/pricing](https://panguard.ai/pricing) for details.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your account">
    If you registered with email, check your inbox for a verification email and confirm your account. Google OAuth users are verified automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
